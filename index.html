Tap to cross

The player slides on one wall, then kicks across the shaft to catch the opposite side while still falling.

Threat lanes

Spikes, saws, arrow traps, and crushers pressure the route with clear warnings and quick restarts.

Risk rewards

Coins, gems, combos, and depth scoring reward clean movement through tighter tower chunks.

Visual target

Dark fantasy arcade, tuned for mobile readability.

The MVP is using procedural placeholder art, but the target is a chunky stone shaft with warm torch accents, glowing purple gems, gold pickups, metal traps, and a small heroic rogue.
